Mission - About Us

J-Town Academy believes that children will flourish in a safe and supportive environment. Our focus is to provide a stimulating early care and education experience which promotes each child's social/emotional, physical and cognitive development through play. Children are supervised by a qualified and caring staff that enjoys interacting with children. Our goal is to provide positive guidance and discipline that encourages children to be independent learners, learn to self-regulate and to develop problem-solving skills. We are committed to providing an educational and fun program in a Christian atmosphere.
